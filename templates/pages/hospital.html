Hospital Solution

Hospitals manage a constant flow of people and urgent situations daily. The diverse environments within hospitals require adaptable security solutions. A robust hospital security system is essential to maintain a safe environment for patients, staff and visitors.

UNV integrated security solution enhances the safety and operational efficiency of the hospital, contributing to better patient care and a more secure working environment for staff.

Around-the-clock uninterrupted protection

- Real-time monitoring of multiple areas such as inpatient wards, outpatient hall, and medical area to prevent potential harm or abuse, and ensuring compliance with safety protocols
- Safeguarding expensive medical equipment and pharmaceuticals from theft or misuse, ensuring that critical resources are available when needed
- Provide hospital people traffic statistics and detect when the number of people in an area exceeds the limit, thereby ensuring the safety of public areas

Protecting healthcare workers and patients

- Support emergency response by providing real-time video feeds to first responders, helping them assess situations accurately and respond appropriately
- Timely notification of various abnormal events such as people falling in the ward, special area intrusion, fighting, smoking, etc.
- Make sure a safety environment and guarantee privacy protection for staff and patient

Reasonable access control management

- Integrating access control subsystem to monitor and manage entry and exit points, ensuring that only authorized personnel have access to sensitive areas such as operating rooms, medication storage, and data centers.
- Monitor and control visitor access, ensuring that visitors are properly identified, and preventing unauthorized individuals from entering restricted areas.
