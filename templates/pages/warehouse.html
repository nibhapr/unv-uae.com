Perimeter Protection

Effective perimeter protection serves as the primary defense against security threats such as break-ins, vandalism, and theft. UNV's robust Tri-guard series and advanced thermal cameras swiftly detect intrusions, fires, and other abnormal, ensuring timely response and safeguarding your premises.

What We Offer

- Smart Intrusion Prevention with high alarm accuracy
- Active Deterrence to intervene proactively
- Thermal Technology to make the invisible visible

Access Control Management

In a bustling warehouse environment with heavy traffic and constant movement of people and products, efficient access control is crucial. UNV offers advanced access control system to facilitate the smooth passage of employees during peak hours and seamless vehicle movements around the clock. Additionally, our visitor management system enhances security by ensuring safe entry and exit for outsiders.

What We Offer

- UNV deep learning algorithm for high accuracy
- ANPR for smooth and automated vehicle access
- Access data available for query and attendance management
