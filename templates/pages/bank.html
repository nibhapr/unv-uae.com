Bank Solution

Personnel

With the growing number of bank branches worldwide, the demand for an integrated security solution has become more urgent than ever. There are various important facilities in the bank, such as ATMs and vault rooms, and the bank would receive a large number of clients everyday. Therefore, a comprehensive bank security solution should take these into consideration and integrate with the actual business of banks. It should cover all critical areas without any blind spot and realize centralized management at headquarters.

Uniview has been dedicated to the security industry for years, offering a mature bank security solution that addresses potential security challenges. Our solution is adaptable to various bank-related surveillance scenarios, ensuring the highest level of security for your institution.

Real-time monitoring and response

With clear and audible video footage available for retrieval and investigation, system empowers swift and effective response measures. Our advanced technology enables the detection of both internal and external threats, proactively preventing suspicious activities before they escalate.

Safe data management and storage

Banks usually need to store large amounts of video and data safely and efficiently to ensure their integrity and accessible archives. Uniview has developed high-performance, highly reliable unified network storage devices for video security. By integrating a wide array of cutting-edge storage technologies, including several patented by Uniview, it can manage large amounts of data, prevent data loss, and ensure robust service stability.

Guarding from a bank branch to the headquarters

UNV's banking solution extends from individual bank branches to headquarters buildings. We offer a unified management platform at the headquarters that oversees all devices across multiple branches, enabling centralized backup of video data, unified management of alarm statistics, and comprehensive data analysis.
